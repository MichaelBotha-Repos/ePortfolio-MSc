About

I've been in the Electrical Engineering field since 2007. From my time at university where we performed embedded programming in C and Assembler I fell in love with programming and computer systems. After my studies I received and apportuinity of employment in the Telecommunications field as a technician, and have remained there since then. My desire to program and master computer systems and the related science has not ceased, and with the chance to redirect my focus through the MSc in Computer Science I can now follow my dream. Object-Oriented Information Systems
(10/08 - 01/11/2021)

Module Learning Outcomes

  • Appraise and evaluate critically the concepts and principles of information systems.
  • Design or modify and document an object-oriented information system using appropriate tools.
  • Develop an object-oriented information system design, implementing this knowledge in applicable programming languages, such as Python and SQL.
  • Develop, implement and evaluate critically information system solutions to facilitate business decisions.
