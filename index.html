About

I've been in the Electrical Engineering field since 2007. From my time at university where we performed embedded programming in C and Assembler I fell in love with programming and computer systems. After my studies I received and apportuinity of employment in the Telecommunications field as a technician, and remained there until November 2021. My desire to program and master computer systems and the related science has not ceased, and with the chance to redirect my focus through the MSc in Computer Science I can now follow my dream of developing software and computer systems on a full-time basis.
